Student Registration Portal

Welcome to the Student Registration Portal! This platform allows you to efficiently register and manage student records with ease. Start by entering a student's details in the form, including their name, student ID, email, and contact number. Once submitted, the student will be added to the record list below.

Edit Functionality: To update any details, click the edit icon with pen button next to the student's record. Modify the necessary information in the form and save the changes.

Delete Functionality: If a record is no longer needed, click the trash icon button to remove it from the list permanently.

The system stores all data in your browser, ensuring that records remain saved even after refreshing the page.
